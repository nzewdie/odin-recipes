Sushi

Description

Sushi is an asian dish made with seaweed,rice, and a source of protein

Ingredents

  • Water
  • white rice
  • rice vineger
  • White sugar
  • salt
  • seaweed
  • crabmeat
  • avacado
  • Cucumber
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Preaheat over ot 300 degrees
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot: stir in rice. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 29 to 25 mins
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Gently stir into cooked rice in the pot and set aside.
  4. lay nori sheets on a baking sheet.
  5. heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1 to 2 minutes
  6. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo shushi mat. use wet hands to spread a thin layer of ric on top. Arange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avacado, cucumber and pickeld ginger over rice in a line down the center. Lift one end of teh mat and rool it tightly over filling to make a complet roo. Repeat with remaing ingredents.
  7. Cut sushi
