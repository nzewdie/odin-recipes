Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is made up of pasta, a surce of protein, and cheese. It is basicly layers of these ingredents repeated over and over until the desired ratio/height is met.

Ingredents

  • Olive Oil
  • Diced Onion
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Parmesan Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Heat olive oil in large skillet in a skilllet.
  2. Lower heat and add marinara sauce
  3. Cook lasagna noodles
  4. Spread botom of pan with meat sauce
  5. Place noodles on top of sauce
  6. add cheese on top of noodles
  7. repeate steps (4-6) until desiered height
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 15 mins
